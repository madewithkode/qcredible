About
In order to curb the impersonation of the office of the SUG public relations, and also the spread of fake news in and around campus, this system was developed to verify authentic publications originating from the above named office.

To verify the authenticity of any publicaton bearing the name of the SUG:

  • Look for a unique digital signature(ID) usually found at the end of every official publicaton
  • Copy the unique ID and visit this portal(https://verify.sugfuto.org)
  • Paste the ID in the verification box provided, and click 'Verify'. You'd get a notification letting you know if the publication with that ID is authentic or not.
Note: If there's no digital signature(ID) on a publication bearing the name of the SUG, don't bother checking, it's not from us.
